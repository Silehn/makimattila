Our Story

The Philosophy

Founded in 2023, Oria was born from a simple desire: to strip away the unnecessary and focus entirely on the essence of fine dining. We believe that true luxury lies in simplicity, quality, and the mastery of fundamentals.

Our kitchen celebrates seasonal ingredients sourced from local purveyors who share our respect for the land. Every dish is a dialogue between tradition and modern technique, designed to let the natural flavors shine.

The space reflects this same ethos. Designed with clean lines, natural materials, and an abundance of light, Oria offers an atmosphere that is simultaneously refined and deeply welcoming.

Purity

Uncomplicated compositions that highlight the exceptional quality of our seasonal ingredients.

Craft

A dedication to precise technique and the continuous refinement of our culinary approach.

Warmth

Hospitality that feels intuitive, gracious, and genuinely connected to every guest.

Meet the Chef

Executive Chef Julian Reyes brings over fifteen years of experience in Michelin-starred kitchens across Europe and North America to Oria. His approach to cooking is profoundly personal, drawing on his heritage while constantly exploring new boundaries.

"Cooking at its highest level shouldn't feel unapproachable," Julian notes. "It should evoke a sense of comfort, a memory, or a surprising moment of joy. At Oria, we are simply curating those moments for our guests."
